About Staalbaek LTD
Staalbaek Ltd was set up in October 2022 as the UK arm of the Organization Stålbæk Holding ApS. For the expansion of the platform Your Footprint. Europe’s most comprehensive CO2eq Calculation tool for the Agricultural Sector.
Developed in conjunction with Denmark’s First CO2eq beef farm Stålbæk Gård ApS.
